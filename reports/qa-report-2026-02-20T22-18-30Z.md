## 7) Findings

### F-003: Payment feature policy too restrictive for embedded wallet contexts
- Severity: Medium
- Evidence:
  - Browser console repeatedly logged: `Permissions policy violation: payment is not allowed in this document.`
  - Triggered from Google Pay wallet frame paths during Stripe Payment Element render.
- Impact:
  - Express wallets may be partially disabled/degraded in embedded checkout contexts.
- Fix applied:
  - Updated `Permissions-Policy` in `_headers` to allow payment feature for Stripe/Google Pay contexts.

## 8) Fixes Applied in This QA Pass

- Updated:
  - `_headers`
    - `Permissions-Policy` changed from:
      - `payment=(self)`
    - to:
      - `payment=(self "https://js.stripe.com" "https://hooks.stripe.com" "https://pay.google.com")`
