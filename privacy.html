Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

This policy explains what Habit Buddy collects, why we collect it, and how you can control your information.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, phone number, and optionally email).
  • Service preferences (habit focus, check-in times, gift details).
  • Message and usage data required to deliver support and troubleshoot service issues.

How We Use Information

  • To deliver daily SMS support and respond to your messages.
  • To operate billing, subscriptions, and gift purchases.
  • To improve product quality, reliability, and user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers needed to operate Habit Buddy (for example, payment processing and CRM operations), under contractual privacy obligations.

Data Retention and Your Rights

We retain data only as long as needed to deliver the service and meet legal obligations. You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting support@tryhabitbuddy.com.

Security

Habit Buddy uses administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information in transit and at rest.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at support@tryhabitbuddy.com.
